About

The PLMUN CITCS Journal is a dedicated blog for students, faculty, and enthusiasts of the College of Information Technology and Computer Studies (CITCS) at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa (PLMUN). This journal serves as a vibrant platform for sharing knowledge, showcasing projects, celebrating achievements, and discussing innovations in the fields of information technology and computer science. Here, readers can explore articles on the latest tech trends, programming tips, research insights, and campus events, fostering a collaborative and inspiring environment for the PLMUN CITCS community.

Our University

Welcome to Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa (PLMun) is a local university in the Philippines. It is one of the two public tertiary education institution located in the City of Muntinlupa

Address: University Road NBP Reservation, Barangay Poblacion, Muntinlupa City, Philippines

College of Information Technology and Computer Studies

The College of Information Technology and Computer Studies (CITCS) at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa (PLMun) is dedicated to equipping students with advanced skills and knowledge in the fields of information technology and computer science. With a focus on academic excellence and practical experience, CITCS offers a range of programs that cover key areas like software development, network administration, data science, and cybersecurity. Guided by a commitment to innovation and industry relevance, the college prepares its students to become proficient IT professionals ready to contribute to the evolving technology landscape and meet the demands of the modern workforce.
