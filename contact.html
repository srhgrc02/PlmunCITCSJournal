About US

The PLMUN CITCS Journal is a dedicated blog for students, faculty, and enthusiasts of the College of Information Technology and Computer Studies (CITCS) at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa (PLMUN). This journal serves as a vibrant platform for sharing knowledge, showcasing projects, celebrating achievements, and discussing innovations in the fields of information technology and computer science. Here, readers can explore articles on the latest tech trends, programming tips, research insights, and campus events, fostering a collaborative and inspiring environment for the PLMUN CITCS community.
