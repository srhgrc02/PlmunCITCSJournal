Cyber Awareness Month

October kicks off National Cyber Security Awareness Month (NCSAM), a crucial campaign led by the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS). This month-long effort is all about spotlighting cyber security best practices and rallying everyone to combat cyber threats....

Cybersecurity Awareness Month is an international initiative that educates everyone about online safety and empowers individuals and businesses to protect their data from cybercrime. Even amidst large-scale data breaches and cyberattacks, Cybersecurity Awareness Month reminds everyone that there are simple, effective ways to keep yourself safe online, protect your personal data, and ultimately help secure our world.

About US

The PLMUN CITCS Journal is a dedicated blog for students, faculty, and enthusiasts of the College of Information Technology and Computer Studies (CITCS) at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa (PLMUN). This journal serves as a vibrant platform for sharing knowledge, showcasing projects, celebrating achievements, and discussing innovations in the fields of information technology and computer science. Here, readers can explore articles on the latest tech trends, programming tips, research insights, and campus events, fostering a collaborative and inspiring environment for the PLMUN CITCS community.
